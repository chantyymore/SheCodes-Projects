My love for fictional books

"Fiction" refers to literature created from the imagination. Mysteries, science fiction, romance, fantasy, chick lit, crime thrillers are all fiction genres.
Examples of classic fiction include

  • To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee
  • A Tale of Two Cities by Charles Dickens, 1984 by George Orwell and Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen

Why i love fiction books?
Fiction gives an escape from reality. When i am tired or have had a long day, reading a fiction book just helps me relax and forget about everything else. Reading it is a great way to distress and boost your mood while improving your vocabulary at the same time.
